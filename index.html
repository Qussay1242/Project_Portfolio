Future Sales Prediction with
Machine Learning

The following project utilizes Python to analyse an advertising dataset and understand the relationship between different variables and the number of units sold. Machine Learning Techniques are utilised to build a model that would give further insights to these relationships

I will take you along the data analysis process starting off with exploratory data analysis such as cleaning and visualizing the data, followed by a correlation test which will allow us to then build a linear regressions model. Finally, we will test the validity of the linear regression model.

WHO COVID-19
Global Data Visualization
using PowerBI

The WHO coronavirus (COVID-19) dashboard presents official daily counts of COVID-19 cases, deaths and vaccine utilisation reported by countries, territories and areas.

Several Datasets were loaded to PowerBI and Data Warehouse principles were applied to produce a functional star Schema. Upon which useful dashboards were built to produce visualizations and extract insights.

About

A little about myself:

The name is Qussay, an individual that enjoys feeding his curiosity by exploring different learning opportunities. Having completed my Bachelors of Science with Honors in Business Management overseas, it became clear to me the necessity of utilising data in businesses. As such I took on different online courses to learn these business tools before stumbling accross the Masters of Business Analytics Programme at the University of Auckland.

Now having returned to New Zealand and currently undergoing the Masters degree, I am taking the opportunity to tackle different projects and learn as much from these experiences when possible. Gradually developing a passion for this field of data one project at a time.

When I'm not busy tackling a dataset or troubleshooting a code, you will find me exploring the beauty of New Zealand, hiking the short walks and tackling the Great Hikes one step at a time.
